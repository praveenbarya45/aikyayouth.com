About Us

"People are at the centre of everything we do."






We are nonprofit team
and work worldwide

Our name - Aikya is a sanskrit word that means Unity or Oneness. The organisation was founded with a deep rooted ambition of pushing our society towards a better self , one that aspires to bring revolution and inspires people to be a part of it.

Core beliefs - We, at aikya believe in the fundamental principles of Indian society, those that have been thriving for centuries . The foundation believes in the values of PACT:

  • Passion - Burning desire to bring change
  • Austerity - Hard work to materialise an idea
  • Compassion - Being humble to fellow beings
  • Truthfulness - True towards your inner self.

    Teaching Program

    Education Support

    The Education Support (EdSupport) programme in our NGO aims to provide quality education to the children in a time-bound manner. Every child needs to be educated and they have got the right to education (RTE) and not every child is fortunate enough. So, with the help of our School-Tuitions and Slum-tuitions, we ensure that every child gets timely education irrespective of the circumstance they live in. Our NGO understands the importance of humanity.

    Slum Tuitions

    According to the 2011-Census data, every eighth urban child in India lives in slums. The children live in deplorable conditions and do not have proper access to education. AIKYA volunteers take the lead and visit the slums weekly and teach the children about best practices on health, hygiene, moral and value-based education and train these children before they could be enrolled in the schools.

    School Tuitions

    AIKYA strives to bring a change by empowering the children with 21st-century skills like Computer Literacy and English language, We also train the children in subjects like Mathematics, Science, General Knowledge, Moral and Value-based Education and Personality development. The volunteers at our NGO make use of self-made Teaching/Learning Materials (TLMs), modals and interactive video-clips to help children learn while having fun and the importance of humanity.


    AIKYA strives to bring a change by empowering the children with 21st-century skills like Computer Literacy and English language with the help of volunteers who act as the change-makers.


    School Initiative


    Back to School Initiative

    The fourth Sustainable Development Goal as put by the United Nations speaks of providing inclusive and equitable quality education to the children and ensure that every child has access to schooling activities. As per the government data, 4.13% was the annual average dropout rate at the primary school level in the country in 2014-15. This means around 10.7 million students dropped out in 2014-15. Our NGO ensures that these children continue their education without dropping out in the midst and aligns its activities to the objectives of the Sustainable Development Goals.We also provide them with the stationery, school bags, school shoes and books to poor children to encourage them and to ensure zero roadblocks in the path of getting knowledge.


    School Transformation Project

    A clean and green environment helps a child best in imbibing best whatever the knowledge s/he gets. At the same time, a school with a full-fledged library, sports equipment and proper infrastructure (toilets, classrooms etc.) motivates a child to learn and also compels the other children to join the schools. At our NGO, our volunteers conduct activities like 'Government School Painting', 'kitchen gardening','establishing libraries', that rejuvenate the school infrastructure. AIKYA has witnessed several stories where the children actively attended the schools as they underwent the School Transformation Project.


    Scholarships


    We believe that every child has the potential to grow and perform well and, in such circumstances, monetary aspects should not pose a roadblock for the child. Every child should haSeveral state governments and the Central government provides scores of scholarships to help children continue their education. AIKYA helps these children in availing the scholarships like National Means Cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS), National Talent Scheme (NTS), etc. We also provide the scholarships to the children which are further classified into Merit-Based Scholarship, provided to the children performing well in their streams and Need-Based Scholarship, provided to the children who cannot continue their education due to monetary constraints and are willing to study further.
